# Chicken Tacos

- Frozen chicken diced or strips
- Southwest spice
- Shredded cheese
- Tortillas
- Chipotle mayo
- Ranch
- Tortilla chips

1. Warm up the chicken in a skillet and season with southwest spice until they are warm and browning.
2. Add chicken, cheese, and sauce to tortillas. Serve with chips.
