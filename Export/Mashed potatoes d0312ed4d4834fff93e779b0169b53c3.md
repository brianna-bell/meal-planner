# Mashed potatoes

- Potatoes
- Butter
- Milk

1. Peel and quarter potatoes
2. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. 
3. Cook for 15 mins or until tender. 
4. Drain water and then add butter and milk. Blend with a mixer and serve.
